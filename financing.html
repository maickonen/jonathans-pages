Common Questions

Financing FAQ

We offer financing through Synchrony Financial and Wells Fargo Financial, lease-to-own through Acima Leasing, and our own in-house layaway program. Each option has different terms and benefits — our team can help you choose the best fit.

We have options for a wide range of credit situations. Acima Leasing offers lease-to-own with no credit needed. Synchrony and Wells Fargo work with various credit profiles. Our in-house layaway requires no credit check at all.

With our layaway program, you make a deposit to reserve your item, then pay the balance over time on a schedule that works for you. There is zero interest and zero risk. If you change your mind, your deposit is returned.

You can apply directly through the form on this page, or visit the Synchrony application portal at mysynchrony.com. You can also apply in-store with help from our team at either location.

Yes — with Synchrony, Wells Fargo, and Acima, approved applicants can take their purchase home the same day. Layaway requires a deposit before the item is reserved for you.

Minimum purchase amounts vary by financing partner. Visit us in-store or call either location and our team will walk you through the specific requirements for each option.
