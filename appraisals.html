Why It Matters

Don't Leave Your Jewelry
Under-Insured

Jonathan's Jewelry is uniquely qualified to appraise your fine jewelry and gemstones. Our experienced staff has the background and gemological training to do a professional job of evaluating the current value of your precious keepsakes.

While these items are priceless to you, it's important to remember that the value of precious metal and gems is constantly changing due to market conditions. Gold, for instance, has seen its value more than double over the past decade.

Accurate appraisals are critical: if your jewelry is ever lost, stolen, or damaged, your insurance company will refer to your appraisal document to settle the claim. An outdated appraisal will likely result in you being under-insured — and that could cost you thousands.

Whether it's an engagement ring you wear every day or a piece you've just inherited from your grandmother's estate, we're ready to offer an appraisal for your security and peace of mind.

Update Every 3–5 Years

Market conditions for precious metals and gemstones shift regularly. Keeping your appraisal current ensures your coverage reflects today's replacement costs — not prices from years ago.
